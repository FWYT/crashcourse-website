CrashCourse is Coming January 18th through 20th.

TartanHacks is on the horizon! Arm yourself with an arsenal of new skills, skills that just may come into play in all your hackathon endeavors next week and beyond! Taught by a host of the most talented Carnegie Mellon students, these 50-minute Crash Courses will set you up with a basic foundation in everything from web development and design to using APIs to Android development. So while the semester is still young and your schedule might still have a teensy bit of space, drop by and pick up the skills that not only will come in handy in future class projects, but also just might be the icing to the cake on your resume—the one you'll be handing out at the EOC next month!
