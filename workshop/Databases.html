Databases

By Vivek Nair

This tech talk is a quick and dirty run down of what databases are, how to work with SQLite to create and update databases and some ways to integrate them into your project. This talk is aimed at people with next to no experience of working with databases and covers enough material to arm them with the basics they need to start developing applications with an external SQL-based database backend. We'll first cover some theory about databases, then move on to how to work with SQL databases with practical demonstrations on a SQLite server. Finally, there will be a demonstration on how to use databases in your applications.
