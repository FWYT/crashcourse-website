Register

To register, head on over to TicketLeap and grab a ticket.

You'll have to change the quantity to “1,” checkout, and then you'll be good to go! Make sure to hang on to your ticket so that we can check you in when you get here.

You'll probably also want to check out the schedule of events and the course description pages (which can be found through the homepage or the schedule) to make sure you've checked everything off the list of prereqs. See you there :D
